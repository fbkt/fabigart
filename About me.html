Professional Work

I am an environmental professional with a Master's degree in Geography, specializing in City and Landscape Ecology from Ruhr-University Bochum. My academic work has centered on dragonfly fauna.

My professional experience covers landscape ecology, species conservation, and environmental planning and I have contributed as an ecological consultant and local species protection program advisor.

Currently, I am leading a company that conducts environmental evaluations and faunistic surveys, ensuring biodiversity considerations are integrated into various development projects. My commitment to the environment extends to volunteer work with NABU Hagen.
